How was this list calculated?

Lists are generated using a Bayesian estimate that pulls data from millions of BeerAdvocate user reviews and normalizes scores based on the number of reviews for each beer.

The general statistical formula is: 
weighted rank (WR) = (v ÷ (v+m)) × R + (m ÷ (v+m)) × C

R = average rating for the beer = (Rating)
v = number of reviews for the beer = (votes)
m = minimum number of reviews required to be considered (currently 10)
C = the mean rating across the dataset (currently 3.64)

    FAQs

    Q: How do your ratings work?

    Learn more about our ratings.

    Q: How are these lists generated?

    See the bottom of this page.

    Q: The ratings seem out of sync?

    The lists are based on weighted rank (WR), which takes the number of Reviews into consideration. As such, the weighted average (Avg) and WR will be different based on the set of beers being viewed.

    Q: Why is a beer with less ratings, but the same WR as another beer, listed first?

    The actual WR may extend several digits past the decimal point. We round the WR for visual purposes.

    Q: Why isn't <insert beer> listed? That's just wrong!

    Maybe it's retired? Maybe it didn't make the cut? If the latter, did you review the beer? If not, then you have even less to complain about.

    Q: How can <insert beer> be better than <insert beer>?

    Ask your first grade math teacher.
